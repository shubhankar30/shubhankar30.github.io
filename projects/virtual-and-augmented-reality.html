Investigation of Virtual and Augmented Reality Gaming Prospects

Publication:

"First Author,"Clash tanks: An investigation of virtual and augmented reality gaming experience", 2017 Tenth International Conference on Mobile Computing and Ubiquitous 2017

Publication link



In this project, we take the first steps to investigate a research combining both AR and VR within one user experience that attempt to leverage the advantages that are enabled by both VR and AR. We developed Clash Tanks, which is a multiplayer game that comprise a VR cockpit and AR Head-Up Display (HUD) to control a real robot.
